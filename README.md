# 🚚 Smart Supply Chain Intelligence System

## 🚀 Overview
Modern supply chains face constant disruptions due to traffic, weather, and operational inefficiencies. This project presents an **AI-powered supply chain intelligence system** that proactively predicts risks, optimizes delivery routes, and provides real-time decision support.

The system acts as a **logistics control tower**, enabling smarter, faster, and more resilient delivery operations.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Global supply chains handle thousands of shipments across complex and volatile environments. Disruptions such as traffic congestion, weather changes, and bottlenecks are often detected too late, leading to delays and inefficiencies.

---

## 💡 Solution
This system:
- Analyzes delivery data in real-time
- Predicts potential disruptions using traffic & weather inputs
- Dynamically optimizes delivery routes
- Provides risk alerts and AI-based recommendations
- Allows scenario simulation for proactive planning

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📦 Route Optimization
- Nearest-neighbor based route planning
- Efficient delivery sequencing

### ⚠️ Risk Prediction Engine
- Considers:
  - Traffic conditions 🚗
  - Weather conditions 🌧️
  - Delivery priority 📦
  - Distance 📏
- Classifies deliveries into Low / Medium / High risk

### 🚨 Disruption Alerts
- Identifies high-risk deliveries
- Provides real-time alerts

### 🤖 AI Recommendations
- Suggests:
  - Alternate routes
  - Additional vehicle deployment
  - Monitoring strategies

### 🌍 Scenario Simulation
- Simulate:
  - Traffic levels
  - Weather conditions
- Perform "What-if" analysis

### 📊 Dashboard Metrics
- Total distance
- Estimated delivery time
- Risk distribution
- System confidence score

### 🚛 Capacity Check
- Ensures vehicle load constraints are respected

### 🗺️ Route Visualization
- Interactive map showing:
  - Delivery points
  - Optimized routes

### 📥 Export Feature
- Download optimized delivery plan as CSV
